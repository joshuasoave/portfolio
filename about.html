About me

Coming up with creative solutions on the fly is my specialty. I recently made a major career transition into Software Engineering after years of working in the travel industry. I believe the key to innovative projects is collaboration. I've loved solving problems and enjoy working on projects with others.

In my time at General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive, I discovered a passion for developing software with Javascript. I really enjoy making responsive designs that look great on mobile devices. If you haven't already, take a look at the projects I've built in my portfolio!
